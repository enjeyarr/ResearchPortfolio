Hi, I'm Nathan

I'm a Design Researcher based in Boston. My current focus is qualitative user research to define and asses design goals, including user testing and ethnography. Anthropology is my academic background, and I'm interesting in breaking down barriers in UX to do more field research and consider cultural patterns and diversity in user needs. My related skills include statisics, market research, front-end coding, and design thinking methods for product strategy. I'm interested in branching out into wireframing and UX Design.
